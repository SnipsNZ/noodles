Ingredients

Noodles (32%) [wheat flour, water, egg white powder, refined soybean oil, salt, raising agent (500), acidity regulator (501), emulsifier (451), colour (160a)], Wonton (32%) [prawn (12%) (Penaeus vannamei) (crustacean) (contains salt, acidity regulators (331, 500)), wheat flour, water, bamboo shoot, refined soybean oil, tapioca starch, sugar, sesame oil, salt, yeast extract, pepper, turmeric], Water, Bok Choy, Chives, Shallot, Salt, Tapioca Starch, Sugar, Yeast Extract, Refined Soybean Oil, Sesame Oil, Pepper.

Cooking Instructions

Microwave — cook from frozen: Remove product from packaging and place in a microwave safe dish. Add 160g hot water and cover with a lid. Heat on high (1000W) for 4.5–5 minutes.

Product to be cooked through before consumption.
