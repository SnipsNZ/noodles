Cooking Instructions

MICROWAVE (cook from frozen): Remove product from packaging and set aside the sachet of fried shallots. Place in a microwave safe dish and cover with a lid. Heat on high (1000W) for 6 minutes. Sprinkle fried shallots over the noodles before serving.

Product to be cooked through before consumption.
