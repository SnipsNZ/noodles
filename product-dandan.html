Ingredients

Ramen Noodles (67%) [wheat flour, water, raising agent (500), colour (160a)], Water, Spinach, Soy Sauce [water, salt, soybeans, wheat, alcohol], Chilli Sauce [water, chilli, garlic, sugar, vinegar, modified tapioca starch (1442), salt], Sesame Oil, Ear Mushroom, Corn Starch, Refined Soybean Oil, Soybean Protein, Chilli Oil [soybean oil, chilli], Sesame Seeds, Sugar, Shallots, Ginger, Garlic, Onion, Sichuan Pepper, Salt.

Cooking Instructions

Microwave — cook from frozen: Remove product from packaging and set aside the sachet of fried shallots. Place in a microwave safe dish, cover with a lid and heat on high (1000W) for 6 minutes. Sprinkle fried shallots over noodles before serving.

Product to be cooked through before consumption.
